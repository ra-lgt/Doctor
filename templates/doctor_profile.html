Personal Profile

Dr. Rahul Bhardwaj MBBS, M.S. is a highly skilled and experienced ophthalmologist with a passion for improving and maintaining optimal eye health. With a focus on precision and a commitment to staying at the forefront of advancements in ophthalmic care, Dr. Rahul Bhardwaj MBBS, M.S. is dedicated to providing the highest standard of care for patients of all ages.

Here Is Skills

As an accomplished Associate Professor and Glaucoma Consultant at TSM Medical College in Lucknow, I bring a wealth of expertise in a spectrum of eye care disciplines. With a primary focus on Glaucoma, Cataract, and Refractive surgery, I am a high-volume proficient Phaco surgeon, adept in the intricacies of Topical, Multifocal, Toric, and premium IOLs. My proficiency extends to a range of surgical techniques, including Small Incision Cataract Surgery (SICS), Phaco with Trabeculectomy, and SICS with Trabeculectomy. I am well-versed in complex glaucoma interventions, such as Trabeculectomy and Glaucoma tube surgeries, employing advanced techniques like Ahmed valve and AADI. Additionally, my expertise encompasses specialized procedures for paediatric glaucoma, such as Trabeculectomy with Trabeculectomy. I am also skilled in Angle surgeries in glaucoma (GATT) and various corrective surgeries, including External DCR, Ptosis correction, Ectropion, and Entropion. With a commitment to precision and a passion for advancing eye care, I also specialize in all-laser procedures in glaucoma. My comprehensive skill set reflects my dedication to providing state-of-the-art eye care solutions, contributing to both academic excellence and patient well-being.
